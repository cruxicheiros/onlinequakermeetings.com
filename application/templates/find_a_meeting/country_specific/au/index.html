Australia Yearly Meeting listing

Australia Yearly Meeting provides a listing for meetings in Australia. It includes a map and a list of meetings by region.

Listings contain contact information for each meeting as well as a link to the meeting's website, if it has one. Many meetings are marked as 'not meeting face-to-face for the time being' and contain information about how to join online meetings.
