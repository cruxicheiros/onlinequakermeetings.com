Quakers in Britain listing

Quakers in Britain provides a search engine and listing for meetings in the UK. You can search by postcode, by the name of a nearby city, or by the name of the meeting house you're looking for.

Listings contain contact information for each meeting as well as a link to the meeting's website, if it has one.

Woodbrooke Quaker Center

Woodbrooke Quaker Center is hosting online meetings for worship several times a week. Some meetings are not hosting their own online meetings, but rather directing attenders to Woodbrooke. They are open to all.
