Meeting house websites

If you already attend a Quaker meeting, a good way to find out if it holds online meetings is to go on your meeting's website. Many meetings have up-to-date websites and will list information about how to join them online there.

Social media

Many meetings keep members up to date using Facebook. If you're struggling to find an up-to-date website for your meeting and have a Facebook account, try searching for the name of your meeting on Facebook and seeing if anything comes up, or checking any related Facebook groups you're already a part of.
