What is this?

A listing of online Quaker meetings to help Quakers find online meetings to attend and spread the word about ones they may be holding.

{% include 'jump_to_directory.html' %}

Find your meeting

Meetings in this directory are listed alphabetically by country, then alphabetically by the name of their meeting. A good way to search this page is by pressing Ctrl+F (Command+F on an Apple computer) and entering a keyword into your browser's search box.

{% include 'back_to_top.html' %}

My meeting isn't here

Lots of meetings that aren't listed here are hosting online meetings. If your meeting has a website, or a social media presence like a Facebook page, those places are good places to check to find an online meeting.

You can get your meeting added to the list or get your listing modified by emailing clarkseanor+oqmd@google.com.

About

This website is under active development. So far, only a few meetings are listed: this is not because this is an exhaustive list of all meetings that are occuring online!

For more information or to get your online meeting listed, email clarkseanor+oqmd@google.com - or if you're more technical go to the GitHub repository and make an issue.
