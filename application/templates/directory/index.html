How to use this directory

Meetings in this directory are listed alphabetically by country, then alphabetically by their names. A good way to search this page is by pressing Ctrl+F (Command+F on an Apple computer) and entering a keyword into your browser's search box.

My meeting isn't here

Lots of meetings that aren't listed here are hosting online meetings. If your meeting has a website, or a social media presence like a Facebook page, those places are good places to check to find an online meeting. Go here for more tips and country-specific advice.

You can get your meeting added to the list or get your listing modified by emailing clarkseanor+oqmd@google.com.

About

This website is under active development. So far, only a few meetings are listed: this is not because this is an exhaustive list of all meetings that are occuring online!
