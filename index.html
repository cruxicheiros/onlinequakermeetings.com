Online Quaker Meetings Directory

What is this?

Because of the current 2019-nCoV pandemic (COVID-19, coronavirus), many Quaker meetings have started meeting online instead of in person. This website exists to help Quakers find an online meeting to attend and spread the word about ones they may be holding.

How to use the directory

Meetings are organised alphabetically by country, then alphabetically by the name of the meeting.
